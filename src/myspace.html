MySpace 2003

Myspace was launched before Facebook. Some could say that Facebook took the idea from Myspace. However, the thing with these two websites is that one did it better then the other. First of all, Myspace was the first social media website I used before Facebook. I remember using it before I began high school. Myspace was so interesting that all of my friends only wanted to visit each others profile to see our template creations. Of course, same as Facebook we could message each other.

The other cool thing Myspace had that Facebook was lacking then it was first released was that we could customize our profile home page and add music. A lot of people always wanted to visit someone else's profile page to see hear their favorite song.

MySpace 2020

Today, Myspace has changed a lot compared to Facebook. It did improved but somehow it is not being used that much like it used to. Although, they have reinvented the website. It is mostly based on music compared to back then.


Their new homepage design looks more appealing then before. They still kept the idea of multiple options to navigate. However, they changed the way their homepage looks a lot. Now it is mostly pictures and promotions. As you scroll you can see artist and videos suggestions. Myspace has become more of a news industry than what it used to be.
