Google 2020

Today, Google is similar to back then, but at the same time better. The static page, just pure white background with a few clickable links makes it so easy to navigate and so easy to read. You don't have to make a website with a lot of design to make it look good. Simpler means less work and better looking.


Now we mostly based anything we need off of Google. It is a scary thing if you thing about it. These websites used to be simple for short information needed. Now we take them too much into consideration when we need anything. We simply search it and that's it. Google simply made anything easier. It is not a bad thing because you can learn anything using Google. That's why it is so important and popular. Anything is almost possible in Google with just a simple click.
