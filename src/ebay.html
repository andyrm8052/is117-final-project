eBay 2020

eBay today has kept almost the same style in their website and the same function. They are still selling the products, but it has expanded. They are still doing auction and in fact it is one of the best one from the E-Commercial industries.


Now eBay looks different than what it looks like before. Everything changes, so no one can expect something to stay the same way. Their website is one of the things that changed. Similar to Amazon, eBay is using the photo/videos ads to attract the customers and click on the product. They have kept the same colors but everything is more organized which is something very important today in order to make someone not leave your website.
