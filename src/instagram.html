Instagram

Instagram 2010

Instagram was another similar social media website to Twitter. However, Instagram could not be used from a computer. You had to download an app and use it on your mobile. Similarly to Twitter when it first was launched, Instagram was another messaging platform. Although, it was more of a picture sharing site.

This is what the website looked like before.


A bit different design template to the other social media websites. They simply had a download button that directed you to a mobile app store. However, they kept a simple design to their homepage.


Instagram 2020

Compared to the other social media platforms, Instagram is new. They have kept their features very similar to when it was first introduced. Same goes for the website design. Now you can use Instagram from a computer. The reason is because websites are becoming more important each day since we have everything we need in one single place.

This is what it looks like now.


The website look almost the same compared to the old one. The only thing they have changed is the color. White is becoming a more reliable color for websites today. It makes things easier to see. It makes other design pop up more.

Instagram in the other hand has kept their way of uploading pictures and commenting the same. unlike Twitter which has become more for sharing stories. Instagram is becoming more of an advertisement site today. If you use the application you will be flooded with people promoting stuff for other websites. Even ads are appearing in your timeline. Things are changing a lot in the social media industry. The main reason is commercial and advertisements.
