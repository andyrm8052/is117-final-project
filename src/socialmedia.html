In this second part I will write about the newest types of social medias. That being said, they all are considered social medias, however, there are some differences between these type of social medias websites compared to the earlier ones. One of these social media website is Twitter. One of the most used social medias website today. Twitter compared to the early social medias websites is mostly based on a fast way of getting information instead of live chatting with someone far away, such as Facebook. The other social media website is Instagram. Well, Instagram is mostly popular on mobiles and was meant to be for them. However, people can still use it via a computer like any other websites.

Instagram is where advertisement takes shape. Instagram can be used for uploading pictures and messaging someone like any other social media website, but today people are using it mostly based on advertisements. You could have a gaming account or a beauty or an art account, you can earn money just by simply promoting their products.

Social media has come a long way and it seems that it will keep growing and become more important each day.
