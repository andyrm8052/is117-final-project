Entertainments

Entertainments in the internet was not popular until the early 2000s. Same as all of the others. The reason was lack of people using the internet or having a computer. Now the entertainment industry in the internet has increase a lot. It has become so popular that even movies retail stores have closed. Many other companies are selling their movies and musics online.

This is where YouTube comes to action. This platform has changed the way we see entertainment in the world. At the beginning of the internet, you could not go to your computer to watch some movies or videos. It was not possible. Now because of YouTube be can do that and even more. People can now share anything they want and even make more from that.

There are many other platform to enjoy any sort of entertainments. One of those is Netflix, which has become so popular today that many other companies are making their own streaming services to compete against them.
