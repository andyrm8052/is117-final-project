Facebook

Facebook 2004

Facebook launched in 2004. Back then, many people did not have internet or computers. Social media was not that big compared to today. Therefore, Facebook was not popular or known.

This is what Facebook used to look back when it first launched.


Back then, Facebook was simpler in a way where there was not a lot to do. You simply register for a new account. Login then message someone if you wanted or upload a picture. There wasn not a lot you could do compared to now. Facebook in my opinion revolutionized the industry and many other companies followed its path.


Facebook 2020

Today, Facebook is use for more than just messaging and uploading pictures.

This is what Facebook looks like now.


You can see that not only the way we use this website have changed, but the style too. Things are becoming more about styles, colors, templates, etc. Facebook when it launched had more details in their pictures. Today Facebook and other websites have simple images. The reason is because things tend to change and they are prefer that way. Simpler design that looks more static. More details means more work and it sometimes makes it harder to look at.

Facebook today is not just about communication. Now we can create groups specifically for anything that we enjoy. If you are a fan of sports, you can join/follow a group that you could read or watch news. There is another reason why social media has changed a lot specially Facebook. Games is the reason and one that since to keep becoming bigger. Back then, Facebook did not have games. Now you can play games thru Facebook or your mobile phone using its account. You can play against others and have a blast. No matter what changes are coming they tend to make it more enjoyable.
