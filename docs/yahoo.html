Yahoo! 1995

Similar to Google, was used and is still being used for searching anything. Although, back there was not a lot you could search. The reason is because there weren't many website. There were not many people posting any information. Yahoo! was before Google. Anything you search today we used to do that on Yahoo!

This is what Yahoo! looked like.


Again, same like the other websites. Many of links that it harder to read. Everything was in the same place it might not look that bad since you had everything in one place you could find stuff easier. However, back in the 90s, websites were not meant to look great.


Yahoo! 2020

Now, Yahoo! is known and competes with Google. Although, Google has taken over the Search Engines platforms. Although, Yahoo has kept their same design aspect by leaving their categories of options in their homepage.


A website that you can find anything and that looks appealing. Compared to Google, Yahoo! kept the newspaper style design. They introduced images and videos in their homepage to get more attention from people visiting. Google kept it simple. However, Yahoo! tends to be better in some other aspects compared to Google and how it used to be. They have created more than just a Search Engine. People can now interact and entertain themselves thru a fantasy sports platform. They can also read and watch videos directly thru Yahoo! Unlike Google which takes you to another website.
