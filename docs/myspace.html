MySpace

MySpace 2003

Myspace launched before Facebook. Some could say that Facebook took the idea from Myspace. However, the thing with these two websites is that one did it better than the other. First, Myspace was the first social media website I used before Facebook. I remember using it before I began high school. Myspace was so interesting that all of my friends only wanted to visit each other's profile to see our template creations. Of course, same as Facebook we could message each other.

The other cool thing Myspace had that Facebook was lacking when released was that we could customize our profile home page and add music. Many people always wanted to visit someone else's profile page to see hear their favorite song.

This below is how Myspace used to look like when it was first launched.


Same as Facebook the design of the page was simple. Although, in Myspace you had many different options to navigate. Such as blog, groups and more.


MySpace 2020

Today, Myspace has changed a lot compared to Facebook. It did improved but somehow it is not being use like before. Although, they have reinvented the website. It is mostly based on music compared to back then.


Their new homepage design looks more appealing than before. They still kept the idea of multiple options to navigate. However, they changed the way their homepage looks a lot. Now it is mostly pictures and promotions. As you scroll, you can see artist and videos suggestions. Myspace has become more of a news industry than what it used to be.
