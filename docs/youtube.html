YouTube

YouTube 2005

YouTube in 2005 was introduced to the world. Not many people could upload their videos like today. In fact people used to simply visit the website and enjoy some short videos.


YouTube website was blank. Not a lot ot enjoy or interact with. The style was simple to navigate to. There wasn't a lot you could do, but that's because it was in the early years.


YouTube 2020

Today, YouTube is the most used streaming platform. People can use it to upload videos, listen to music. Watch any sort of video or even movies. Anything related to entertainment, YouTube has it. It has taken the Entertainment industry to another level. It has no competition and that is why it is still in the top.


Similar to many websites today. The design is to make you interact with other things. To attract your attention and make you click on a thumbnail. Before we couldn't do that. Today everything is based on advertisements. Anyone can upload a video and make money from it. Of course is not that simple. Although, by uploading a trending video with a eye-catching thumbnail you can attract attention.

Anything done in YouTube is free. You don't even need an account to watch a video. In fact, YouTube is becoming so popular that they implemented live television. Everything is moving into the internet today. They even have their own film studio where they make their own shows or movies.
