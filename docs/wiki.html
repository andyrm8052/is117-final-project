Wikipedia

Wikipedia 2001

Wikipedia in the early 2000s was mostly used for finding definitions. It wasn't always like that. Although, people only visited the sites to find any quick information about something. Similar to the Search Engines, there was not a lot you could search back then.


Wikipedia has always been used. Although, since there are so many website specially Search Engine website that you can search anything, Wikipedia has lost many visitors. I used to use it back when I was in high school. Now we can do anything thru Google. Although, Wikipedia for me is still great for definitions and information about places and people.

Their website homepage looks similar to any other website back in the 90s or early 2000s. Having a lot of links centered in the homepage was popular. Although, now it tends to be a bit too much.


Wikipedia 2020

Encyclopedias today are on another level like many other websites and industries. More things start to come to the internet which means more information is gathered and used. We used to go to the libraries to read a book. Now we can go to an online Encyclopedia like Wikipedia to read a short summary of anything and you immediately will know about it without buying and just by simply reading for a couple of minutes.


Their website is better looking. Similar to that of Google. It is simple with a limited amount of links. The only things we do is type anything we want to know about and in a matter of seconds we can read about it without having to rent, buy or read a whole book.
