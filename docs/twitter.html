Twitter

Twitter 2006

Twitter was launched in the year 2006. A year where multiple social media websites were being used. Anyone could think that it had a lot of competition which was true. However, Twitter has taken social media to another level. That's why I made a second part dedicated to social medias such as Twitter.

Below is how Twitter used to look like before.


Same as any other website, back then the design and style didn't look that good. People are more creative now and since technology has advanced the design of websites has improved. Twitter should not be compared to Facebook or Myspace. The reason is simple. Twitter is more of a social media type news industry merged together. You can see that there are errors in some cases such as misplaced text or an image is too stretched.


Twitter 2020

Well, people do upload images and comment them similar to any other social media website. However, it is not really that popular with Twitter. Mostly people use it for news and for posting their daily routine and much more.

This is how Twitter looks like today.


A more pleasant design to look at. Websites today tend to keep it simple with minimum amount of colors. The reason is to keep a simple template you can interact and see without any difficulties. Comparing it to when it first was launched, Twitter took the idea of using a new method of trending something. They made the "#" relevant and important. We can see them in the homepage. Anything trending at the moment.It allows people to find what they are looking for faster. There is another reason why Twitter today is so popular. A lot of famous people prefer to use Twitter because of the previous reasons. It is also more genuine than before.
